Tap the Top Right-most Button to look at your Options.

Ping Pong

Instructions :

  • You and Computer have to bat against the ball.
  • One who missed the ball will end up giving a point to the opponent.
  • One who scores 5 points first will be the winner.

Note: Ball speed increases with Time !!

Simon Game

Instructions :

  • You have to remember the sequence of colours tapped.
  • If you forget the sequence, game will be over.
  • So, let's test your memory.

Rock Paper Scissor

Instructions :

  • You and Computer have to among Rock, Paper and Scissor.
  • Rock wins over Scissor.
  • Scissor wins over Paper.
  • Paper wins over Rock.
  • Choose wisely!!

Let's Practice Tables

Instructions :

  • Can you memorize multiplicative tables?
  • You have to check the equation by tapping right or wrong.
  • At the end you'll get your performance score.
